A Peek into the Past

An insight to MIT Student House's active social culture

As a student group, we have been featured in Technique, MIT's yearbook, since our earliest years. The house became co-ed in 1969, the first college co-ed living space in the entire East Coast, as you can see in the yearbook photos.
