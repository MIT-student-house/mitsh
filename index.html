Hi, We are the MIT Student House!

Founded in the fall of 1930, we are a non-Greek independent living group (ILG) in Kenmore Square, on the Boston side of the Charles, housing about 22 students.

Why Student House?

Great Location

Located within walking distance from MIT Campus with regular shuttle service.

Affordable

Members share house chores, so the cost of housing is significantly cheaper.

Social Events

We have an active culture of social events all year round.
