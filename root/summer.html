Summer Housing

Prioirty Deadline : April 15, 2018

Application Form

Housing Period for Summer 2018

Move-in date : May 27 for MIT Students and June 9 for 9 MIT Students.
Move-out date : August 26

Important Note
We are a student run co-op house, not the official MIT summer housing office, which is under the MIT Division of Student Life.

Perks

  • Air conditioned rooms and common areas.
  • Fully furnished rooms.
  • Professional cleaning in public areas
  • Utilities and Internet (Wi-Fi and Ethernet).
  • Free laundry.
  • Fully equipped commercial kitchen, fridge, freezer, espresso machine, waffle-maker.
  • Optional meal plan, or cook for yourself.

Eligibility

Our summer housing has fewer restrictions. You have to be a college student, although not nescessarily an MIT Student. Also, we only house college (4-year and 2-year) and graduate students.

Fill out the application form by April 15. Applications submitted after this deadline will still be considered, space permitting.

Fees

The following fees are for the entire summer housing period. Even if you are not staying for the entire period, you have to pay the whole rent.

2018 Rent (per person)
  • Security Deposit: $600 (to be paid within a week of being offered a spot)
  • Double: $2500
  • Triple: $2300
  • Quad: $2100
