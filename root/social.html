Social Events

An insight to MIT Student House's active social culture

Social events are central to Student House. We have weekly study breaks (with snacks and ice-cream :) ) every Wednesday to take a break from academics and socialize when the week is most hectic. In addition, we host house parties every three Fridays, with delicious home-made snacks and various drinks. We also host a formal every semester, a picture of which you can see above. Especially important would perhaps be the spring formal, during which we honor our graduating members.

If you are interested in knowing more about the house, please feel free to stop by our social events, in addition to our house dinners (every Sun-Thu at 8pm)!
